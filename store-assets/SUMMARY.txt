🎯 QUICK START GUIDE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

STEP 1: READ THE DOCS (10 minutes)
──────────────────────────────────
  1. Open: QUICK-REFERENCE.md ← Quick overview
  2. Then: STORE-ASSETS-GUIDE.md ← Full details

STEP 2: CREATE SCREENSHOTS (1–2 hours)
───────────────────────────────────────
  A. Open app in Android emulator at 1080×1920
     └─ Create 5–6 screenshots showing main features
     └─ Save to: google-play-store/screenshots/

  B. Open app in iOS simulator at 1242×2688
     └─ Create 5–6 screenshots showing main features
     └─ Save to: apple-app-store/screenshots/

  C. Optional: Create 15–30 sec video for Apple App Store
     └─ Save to: apple-app-store/screenshots/

STEP 3: UPLOAD TO STORES (30 minutes)
──────────────────────────────────────
  A. Google Play Console:
     └─ Upload icon, feature graphic, screenshots

  B. App Store Connect:
     └─ Upload icon, marketing artwork, screenshots

  C. Both stores:
     └─ Add app description, keywords, privacy policy

STEP 4: SUBMIT FOR REVIEW
──────────────────────────
  A. Google Play: ~2–4 hours review time
  B. Apple App Store: ~24–48 hours review time
